   :Parameters:

       **drill_to_layer** : :class:`python:str`
           Layer name where the backdrill stops.

       **diameter** : :class:`python:str`
           Backdrill bore diameter, e.g. ``"0.25mm"``.

       **stub_length** : :class:`python:str` or :data:`python:None`, :obj:`optional`
           Stub length beyond the target layer.  ``None`` means no stub.

       **drill_from_bottom** : :ref:`bool <python:bltin-boolean-values>`, :obj:`optional`
           ``True`` (default) drills from the bottom side.
